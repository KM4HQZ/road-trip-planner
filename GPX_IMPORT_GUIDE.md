## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Best Results

### Before You Go
1. **Download offline maps** in your navigation app for areas without cell service
2. **Save important phone numbers** from GPX waypoints (hotels, vets)

### While Traveling

3. **Keep vet info handy** just in case of pet emergencies

### Navigation Options
- **Follow the exact route**: Use the track for the planned scenic drive
- **Navigate to waypoints**: Let the app calculate the fastest route between stops
- **Explore POIs**: Tap waypoints to see hotels, restaurants, and attractions

## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### "File format not supported"

- Try opening with a different app

### "No route displayed"
- Check if the app needs offline maps downloaded

- The GPX includes many POIs - use app filters to show only what you need
- Most apps let you toggle POI categories on/off
